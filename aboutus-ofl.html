About Ogden Family Law

Established 2018

Located in Austin, Texas, Ogden Family Law is made up of 2 partners and around 10 other assistants, paralegals, or other employees in the firm. As a family law firm, we provide services and legal aid to those in the Austin area on legal matters such as: divorce, custody, estates & wills, prenuptial agreements, spousal support, and more.


Our Values

Here at Ogden Family Law, we strive to handle every case with our core values in mind. We understand the sensitivity of the issues we handle, and that’s why we factor in these ideals when handling any case. All of our employees practice approcahability, capability, thoughtfulness, sincerity, and honesty.
